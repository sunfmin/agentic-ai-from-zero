# Weekly Standup Summary

It can be reported that progress has been made on several fronts this week, with notable achievements being recorded in the area of the migration project as well as in the work that is being done on the customer onboarding flow improvements.

The migration team was successful in completing the cutover of three additional services to the new infrastructure, bringing the total number of migrated services to twelve, with seven remaining to be addressed over the course of the next two sprints. Blockers were encountered with the authentication layer, but those blockers have now been resolved through collaboration with the platform team.

The onboarding work continues to move forward, although some delays have been experienced due to dependencies on the design team's bandwidth being more constrained than was originally anticipated when the timeline was first put together. The team is exploring options for parallelizing certain workstreams in order to mitigate the impact of these constraints.

Looking ahead to next week, the priorities will be focused on the completion of the migration work and the kickoff of the next phase of onboarding improvements.
