# Product Launch — Draft Post

The launch of our new product was successfully completed by the team yesterday, and feedback from customers has been received which suggests that the response has been positive across multiple dimensions, although a small number of issues were identified by the support team which are currently being looked into by engineering and which we expect to be resolved by the end of the week.

A retrospective will be conducted next week in order to capture learnings from the launch process, and the outputs of that retrospective will be shared more broadly so that other teams within the organization can benefit from what was learned. Things that could be improved next time include the timing of communications, the level of preparation for customer support, and the way decisions were made about which features to ship.

The team is to be thanked for their hard work over the last quarter. Without their efforts, the launch would not have been possible.
