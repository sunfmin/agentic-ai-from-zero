# Fixture Draft — Q4 Planning Kickoff

We are pleased to announce that the kickoff of our Q4 planning process is being scheduled for next week, and as part of our broader commitment to operational excellence, a number of important workstreams have been identified which will require the engagement of all team members in order to ensure successful delivery against our stated objectives.

The optimization of our planning cadence has been identified as a key focus area, with the goal being to achieve a meaningful reduction in the amount of time that is currently being spent on planning activities while at the same time improving the quality of the outputs that are being produced. A working group is in the process of being formed for the purpose of evaluating various options.

It should be noted that participation in the kickoff is expected from all team leads, and pre-reads will be circulated by the end of the week.
